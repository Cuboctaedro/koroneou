Biography:

Born 1957, in Athens, Greece
Lives and works in Volos and Athens, Greece 
Professor of Art and Environment at the Dept. of Architecture, University of Thessaly


### Education

1981-1986 École Nationale Supérieure des Beaux-Arts (ENSBA), Paris


### Awards

2003 Winner of the DESTE prize, DESTE Foundation for Contemporary Art
