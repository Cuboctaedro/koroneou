Title: Helmut Middendorf

----

Type: represented

----

Biography:

Born 1953 in Dinklage, Germany
Lives and works in Berlin and Athens

1973-1979 Academy of Fine Arts, Berlin, class of K.H Hodicke
1977 Founding member of the ‘Galerie am Moritzplatz’, (A gallery of artists in Berlin-Kreuzberg. The painters of the gallery formed the group of the ‘New Fauves’)
1979 Teacher for experimental Film at the School of Fine Arts in Berlin
1981 Scholarship from the DAAD for New York
