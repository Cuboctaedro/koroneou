Title: George Condo

----

Type: exhibited

----

Biography:

Born in 1957 in Concord, New Hampshire, USA
Lives and works in New York

### Education

1976-78 Bachelor of Fine Arts Lowell University, Massachusetts
2004 Visiting Lecturer on Visual and Environmental Studies, Harvard, Boston, MA
