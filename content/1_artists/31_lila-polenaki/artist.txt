Biography:

Born 1964, in Athens
Lives and works in Athens

### Education

1982-1985 Law studies at the University of Athens
1985-1986 School of Fine Arts, Thessaloniki
1986-1987 School of Fine Arts, Athens
1987-1993 School of Fine Arts UDK, Berlin
1994 Master of Fine Arts, with Prof. Berndt Koberling, UDK, Berlin
1994-1996 Scholarship, NaFoG postgraduate grant, UDK, Berlin
