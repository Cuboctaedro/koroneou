Title: Yuzheng Cheng

----

Type: exhibited

----

Biography:

Born 1977, in Suzhou, China
Lives and works in Berlin

### Education

1995-1998 Interior Design at the School of Architecture Nanjing, China
2000-2001 Moves to Germany. Studies Graphic Arts at the Muthesius-School of Arts and Design, Kiel
2001-2005 Studies Painting under Prof. KH Hodicke at the University of Arts, Berlin 
2005 Master scholar under Prof. KH Hodicke at the University of Arts, Berlin. Prize-winner of the Theodorus-Art-Prize 2005, awarded by the president of the University of Arts, Berlin
